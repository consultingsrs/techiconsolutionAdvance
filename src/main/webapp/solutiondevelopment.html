Solution Development

Tech Icon solutions provides end-to-end software solutions with its expert team of engineers who have rich experience working with TIER1, TIER2 technology companies and state of the art technologies.

We follow various engineering project life cycle models for the solutions we provide. After understanding the customer requirements, time to market and other attributes, parameters involved for the end delivery we choose the appropriate project life cycle model with the lowest cost possible. Our project life cycle model is designed to accommodate the fast paced market needs and changing customer requirements. This is achieved through a hybrid approach of the standard models practiced in the industries.

We not only understand customer end requirements and implement to satisfy their needs to perfection, but also provide suggestions to improve the competitiveness of their product/solutions in the market. This is attained through technical expertise, market expertise; laser focused technical and flexible project planning.

The cost model followed are in two ways.

  • project based cost model
  • effort based cost model

We follow the strict IPR practices followed in the industry. Our team of professionals are specially trained to adhere to the practices followed.
