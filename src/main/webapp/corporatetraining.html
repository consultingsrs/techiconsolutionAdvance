Corporate Training

As the needs of the customer change and as threat perception from competition increases, technology adoption is of critical importance to companies worldwide. In a highly competitive 'flat-world' marketplace, every company faces the challenges of improving productivity of its employees to ensure that its customers' needs are served on-time and within budget. Investing on employee training is one of those smart decisions which not only provides highest returns but also serves the long term interests of the company.

But in choosing education and training providers, it's important to make sure the money you're investing translates into tangible results: increased employee productivity, improved performance at their jobs, higher morale, greater retention and reduced recruiting costs. In the end, education and training must give your business a competitive edge. We understand these needs and have responded by designing, collecting and improving upon the metrics that matter most to you.

  • Our training yields a 4.3:1 benefit-to-cost ratio relative to predicted increases in job performance.
  • Over 97% of our clients utilize knowledge and skills gained in the classroom on the job.
  • And more than 50% of performance improvements realized on the job are directly attributable to the training obtained in our classrooms.

As experts in creating learning programs for knowledge workers, Tech Icon Solution Inc has helped hundreds of customers achieve better business results through training every year. We continue to expand our offerings, locations, and learning methods to meet the growing demands placed on organizations and their employees.
