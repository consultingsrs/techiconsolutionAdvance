SMART ODC

One of the major management goal for the software organizations is to cut the cost involved in maintaining the research and development teams. To achieve this goal, the trend is to create an offshore development centre under the same organization. But, the procedural and managerial overhead in setting up and maintaining these teams at a distant location absorbs considerable time and cost. This may in turn affect the time to market of the related products also.

To address this concern, techicon solutions provides an alternative solution called Smart ODC.

Smart ODC is a technique where a product development/maintainenance cycle is performed under the highly skilled dedicated team created and offered by the techicon solutions. Though this team will be part of techicon solutions, their tasks will be determined and managed directly by the customer

Below are the key advantages techicon solutions provide in having a offshore development centre (ODC) in India

  • Considerably lesser cost of ownership
  • Relieves customer from the hassles involved in setting up an offshore development centre and team hirings.
  • Dedicated Research and Development units to satisfy the customer requirements.
  • Deployment of highly skilled resources and need base consultants are provided by the techicon solutions
  • By setting up the ODC at a geographical location with different timezone, customers will get benefitted by having 24 x 7 product development/maintenance cyle and resource sharing. This effectively helps in time to market of the product.
  • The difficulty arises in team interaction due to the varying geographical time zones; reduce the efficiency of project management. Our ODC solution reduces this inefficiency by creating the team that can work in a overlapping time zones.the team that can work in a overlapping time zones.
